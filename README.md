# style-nest
**StyleNest** is an AI-powered fashion styling assistant that helps users generate outfit ideas, color palettes, styling tips, wardrobe combinations, and sustainable redesign concepts. Designed for fashion students and everyday users, the app offers a modern, creative, and beginner-friendly experience for exploring personal style.

StyleNest 👗✨
“Grow Your Style, Your Way.”

Overview :
StyleNest is an AI-powered fashion styling and design assistant created for fashion students, beginner stylists, and everyday users. The app helps users overcome creative block by generating outfit ideas, color palettes, styling suggestions, wardrobe combinations, and sustainable fashion solutions.
The goal of StyleNest is to make fashion styling easier, more creative, and more accessible through a modern and beginner-friendly mobile experience.
